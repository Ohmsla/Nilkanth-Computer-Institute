About Us

Welcome Nilkanth Computer Institute of Information Technology

Nilkanth Computer Institute of Information Technology is an ISO 9001:2015 Certified Organization & Registered Ministry of Education India Govt. of India & Department of higher education statics division, New Delhi & Nilkanth Welfare Education Samiti (NWES) Uttar Pradesh India. The Institute is established several years ago and it is one of the leading computer institutes in district. We have created a very friendly learning environment with the latest computer hardware and software technologies. Whether you are new to the computer industry or are looking to improve your skills, we have the right course for you.
Well-qualified, experienced certified instructors train the participants with easy-to-use step by step training material in the most optimized period of time. Nilkanth Computer Institute of Information Technology offers more than 10 computer courses in the field of Accounting, Multimedia & Animation, Software, Hardware and Networking, CAD, Mobile Technology, Spoken English, Data Entry and E-Typing Programs to fulfill the student with Computer literacy to avail better employment.
The institute also offers vocational (summer camp) training programs for School children using Computers in Accounting, Multimedia, Hardware and Networking, Spoken English etc. The various vocational courses are offered through Certificate courses, Diploma courses, Advanced Diploma Courses and Masters Diploma courses in subjects related to the use of computers. These vocational courses are constantly updated according to the current trend in the IT industry. Training is conducted in comfortable and spacious labs equipped with computers which are constantly updated with the latest technology.
The Institute also provides Soft Skills Training in Communication Skills, Language Skills, Personality Development, Interview Facing Skills, Group Discussion, Competitive Exam Facing Skills and Job Training.
